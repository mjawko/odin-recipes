Vegan Karaage

Let's use tofu to make vegan fried chicken!

Cooking time 25 minutes (prep 2 days)

Ingregients

  • Tofu(400g)
  • Oil(until cover the tofu in your pan)
  • Flour(50g)
  • Salt(5g)
  • Soy sauce(50g)
  • Garlic(2 pieces)

Steps

  1. Freeze tofu over night.
  2. Put it in your refreigitor until the texture of the tofu turn into sponge.
  3. Squeeze it and reduce the water from the tofu.
  4. Mix salt, soy sauce and garlic in a container.
  5. Soak Tofu into the mixed seasonings over night.
  6. Cover marined tofu with flour and fry it.
